The RCJA Privacy Statement (Privacy Statement) sets out RCJA's policies in relation to the management of your personal information. 2. Collection
RCJA collects personal information (Information) about students, mentors, members and other people in order to perform its functions and activities. Information may be collected before, during and after the course of persons association with RCJA.

3. Use and disclosure
RCJA will use Information it collects from you for the primary purpose of collection, and for such other secondary purposes that are related to the primary purpose of collection and reasonably expected, or to which you have consented.
Examples of purposes for which RCJV might use or disclose Information:
- Research by RCJA's affiliates.
- Marketing by RCJA's affiliates, however student information will not be disclosed for marketing purposes.

4. Access and correction
You are encouraged to ensure the information you supply to RCJA is kept up-to-date. If you know that details have changed, please let us know. Should you wish to update your Information, please contact RCJA at the address listed in point 5.

5. Security of Information
RCJA takes reasonable steps to protect the Information it holds from misuse and/or loss and unauthorised access, modification and disclosure, including through the use of security certificates and password protection for access to electronic records.

6. Inquiries
Should you have any questions in relation to the RCJA Privacy Statement, please contact RCJA via mail. Our registered address is 117 Clifton Springs Road, Drysdale, Victoria 3222.
